Frequently Asked Questions

Get Every Single Answers There if you want

  • How often should someone get checkups and cleanings?

    Our team (along with the American Dental Association) recommends that everyone should have at least two checkups and cleanings per year to help prevent the most common dental problems. Some may require more than two depending on their needs, but for most, one preventive visit every six months is plenty to keep their smile protected.

  • What should I do about my missing teeth?

    VR Dental Design offers a number of solutions for any degree of tooth loss, but for the most natural-looking and longest-lasting results, we typically recommend dental implants. Thanks to having an in-house periodontist, we’re able to offer all-inclusive implant care right here in Merrimack, making it easier than ever for someone to rebuild their smile for a lifetime.

  • Can you make my smile more attractive?

    Yes! We have a selection of cosmetic dentistry treatments that are specifically designed to help patients achieve their perfect smile! Whether you simply want whiter teeth or are interested in a complete makeover, we can help you have the kind of smile that always fills you with confidence.

  • Going to the dentist makes me nervous! Can you help?

    With sedation dentistry, we can ensure that even the most anxious patient has a relaxing and stress-free dental experience. We can just “take the edge off” with nitrous oxide or even help you sleep through your entire appointment with general anesthesia. In the end, we’ll make sure going to the dentist is nothing to dread anymore.

  • Will you take my insurance?

    Yes! We accept all insurance plans and work hard to maximize our patients’ coverage at every visit. We’ll make saving on your dental care one of the easiest parts of your appointments! To learn how we can use your particular plan, be sure to give us a call today.
